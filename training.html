Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from Savage Society?

Savage Society is your all-access pass to workouts, community, and monthly programming. It's self-guided — you choose the programs and follow the plan.

1-on-1 Coaching is fully customized. I design your training and nutrition plan specifically for your body, goals, and schedule. You get direct access to me, bi-weekly calls, and hands-on coaching.

Think of it this way: Savage Society = Netflix (unlimited content, you choose). 1-on-1 Coaching = Private chef (everything is made for you).

Do I need to be experienced to work with you?

No. I work with beginners all the time. However, 1-on-1 coaching is a significant investment, so I recommend trying Savage Society first if you're brand new to lifting. Once you have a foundation, 1-on-1 coaching will take you to the next level.

Can I do this from home, or do I need a gym?

I can program for either. If you have a full gym, I'll design a plan to maximize that equipment. If you're training at home with limited equipment, I'll work with what you have. Just let me know in your application.

What if I have an injury or limitation?

I program around injuries all the time. Always consult your doctor before starting a new program, but I'll work with you to design a plan that's safe and effective for your body.
