Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from Savage Society?

Savage In 6 is a 6-week intensive challenge with a specific start/end date, cohort-based accountability, and a clear transformation goal. It's designed for rapid results and community motivation.

Savage Society is an ongoing membership with unlimited access to workouts, monthly programs, and community — no specific start/end date. It's self-paced and flexible.

Many women start with Savage In 6 to kickstart their transformation, then join Savage Society to continue training long-term.

What happens after the 6 weeks?

You'll have the option to join the next Savage In 6 cohort (with a different goal), transition to Savage Society for ongoing training, or apply for 1-on-1 coaching.

Many women complete multiple Savage In 6 challenges throughout the year — Savage Gains in spring, Savage Shred in summer, etc.

Do I need experience to join?

No. All three challenges include beginner-friendly modifications. If you're brand new to lifting, we recommend starting with Savage Lifestyle to build a foundation.

If you have training experience and want to push hard for 6 weeks, choose Savage Gains or Savage Shred based on your goal.

What's the difference between Lite and Full?

Lite gives you the core challenge experience: training program, nutrition guidelines, and community access.

Full adds live coaching sessions with Xenia, personalized form checks, macro adjustments, accountability partner matching, and a swag pack. It's the complete VIP experience.
