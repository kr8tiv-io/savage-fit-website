What is Savage?

Savage Fit is an online coaching community led by IFBB Figure pro Xenia Busigin to help you get fit and smash your fitness goals.

We use a fitness application which gives you your workouts with video demonstrations and allows you to track your progress in real time.

What is Savage Subscription?

Savage subscription is a self guided subscription to all of the workout libraries allowing you to select workouts that are appropriate for your goals.

You can change your program anytime by messaging Xenia and track your workouts, meals, weight, and photos in the app.

This doesn't include weekly check ins but does include access to our facebook community where you can ask questions and keep each other motivated. Xenia checks in often.

You can purchase a custom meal plan from Xenia at an additional cost if you choose.

What is Savage Subscription?

What are Fitness Challenges?

Fitness Challenges are seasonal six to nine week sprints where the group participates in a custom workout to keep each other motivated.

Savage Subscription VIPs get 30 percent off of meal plans for challenges and free access to challenge workouts which automatically are dropped into the app.

What are Fitness Challenges?

How Does Online Coaching Differ?

Online Coaching from Xenia is a customized program, meal plan, and diet plan with weekly check ins via email to Xenia reporting and getting feedback on your progress and challenges.

It is a completely inclusive experience where everything is customized based on a questionnaire and your coaches are available to answer questions when you have them via email or messaging.

It is a fully catered coach experience. No cookie cutter. No BS. No recycled programs. Your body, your needs, your goals.
