Frequently Asked Questions

I've never lifted weights before. Is Savage Society for me?

Absolutely. Savage Society includes beginner-friendly programs designed specifically for women who are new to lifting. You'll get step-by-step video tutorials with coaching cues, form breakdowns for every exercise, and beginner progression plans.

You don't need to know how to lift when you join. That's what Xenia is here to teach you.

Can I do these workouts at home, or do I need a gym?

Both! Savage Society includes:

  • Gym-based programs (full equipment access)
  • Home workout programs (minimal equipment — dumbbells, resistance bands, bodyweight)
  • Hybrid programs (mix of both)

You'll filter by equipment availability when choosing your program. No gym? No problem.

I want to lose fat AND build muscle. Can I do both?

Yes — and that's the entire point. Body recomposition (losing fat while gaining muscle) is 100% possible, especially for beginners and intermediate lifters.

Savage Society programs are designed to build lean muscle through progressive strength training and burn fat through a slight caloric deficit (WITHOUT starvation). You'll get the exact training + nutrition framework to make it happen.

How long until I see results?

Here's the honest answer:

  • Strength gains: 2-4 weeks (you'll feel stronger before you look different)
  • Visible muscle definition: 6-8 weeks (if you're consistent with training + nutrition)
  • Significant body transformation: 12-16 weeks (this is when people start asking what you're doing)

The key: Consistency. Show up. Trust the process. Don't quit after 3 weeks.
